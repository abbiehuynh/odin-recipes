Oreo Balls

Description

Oreo balls may seem like an intimidating treat to make,

but they are not as difficult as they may look to be!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of regular Oreos
  • 8 oz softened cream cheese
  • 14 to 16 oz almond bark or melting wafers

How to Make Oreo Balls

  • Oreo Ball
  1. Break Oreos in half and add to a food processor to crush into a fine texture
  2. Mix crushed Oreos in a large bowl with the softened cream cheese
  3. Use a tablespoon to scoop the Oreo mixture and roll into balls
  4. Place Oreo balls on to wax paper
  • Icing
  1. In a mixing bowl, melt half of the almond bark in the microwave in 30 second increments until its completely melted.
    • Stir in between increments
    • Should take about 90 seconds
  2. Place one ball into the almond bark using a spoon and coat evenly
  3. Lift the Oreo ball out of the chocolate and allow the excess to run off
  4. Place Oreo ball on tin foil or wax paper
  5. Allow the chocolate to harden for about 10 minutes

How to Store Oreo Balls

  • Airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days
  • Airtight container in the frigde for up to 2 weeks
  • Gallon freezer bag in the freezer for up to 3 months
