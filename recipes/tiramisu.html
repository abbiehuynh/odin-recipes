Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is an Italian dessert that is filled with rich creamy layers.

Using a traditional Italian recipe by Vanessa Martin, Nagi has created a chef's recipe to show us how to make tiramisu the proper Italian way!

Ingredients

  • Lady fingers
    • or Pavesini,
    • Savoiardi biscuits
  • Mascarpone
  • Frangelico
    • or Kahlua,
    • other liquor
  • Coffee
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar
  • Eggs

How to Make Tiramisu!

  1. Separate the egg whites from the egg yolks
  2. In Bowl One:
    • Mix egg yolks with sugar until creamy
    • Add mascarpone and vanilla extract and mix again until creamy
  3. In Bowl Two:
    • Mix egg whites until fluffy
  4. Mix the components of Bowl One and Bowl Two into one bowl until creamy
  5. Make coffee espresso
    • Pour espresso into a large, wide bowl or plate
    • Add Frangelico into bowl/plate
  6. Lady Fingers in espresso mix
    • Place Lady Fingers in baking dish
    • Add a layer of cream on top of Lady Fingers
  7. Repeat Step 6 until baking dish is filled
